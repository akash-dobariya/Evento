The Problem

The Industry is Broken

The event-volunteer sector suffers from fragmented, informal coordination — leading to frequent no-shows, payment disputes, and zero accountability.

Chronic No-Shows

Last-minute cancellations with zero backup systems. Organizers are left scrambling, events suffer, and trust evaporates.

Payment Disputes

No verified payment tracking creates a breeding ground for fraud, delayed payments, and broken promises between parties.

Zero Accountability

Informal WhatsApp coordination with no records, no enforcement, and no consequences. Anyone can ghost without repercussions.

Built for Trust

EVENTO replaces unorganized WhatsApp groups with a transparent, rule-driven digital platform. Centralized applications, verified users, and enforced accountability.

Two-Way Accountability
