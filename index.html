THE PROBLEM

The event-volunteer sector suffers from fragmented, informal coordination, leading to frequent no-shows, payment disputes, and a total lack of accountability.

No-Shows

Frequent last-minute cancellations with no backup system in place.

Payment Disputes

No verified payment tracking leads to fraud and delayed payments.

Zero Accountability

Informal WhatsApp coordination with no records or enforcement.

THE INNOVATION

This innovation replaces unorganized WhatsApp groups with a transparent, rule-driven digital platform. By centralizing applications and verifying users, it ensures accountability, fair payments, and professional reliability.

Verified Payment Guards

Two-Way Accountability

Centralized Dispute Resolution

Review-Based Trust
