Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to EVENTO. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform designed to connect volunteers and event organizers with transparent, verified, and accountability-first workflows.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a trustworthy and transparent ecosystem, we collect two categories of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, role type (Volunteer or Event Organizer), and optional profile details.
  • Platform Activity Data: Check-in/check-out logs, verified attendance records, trust ratings, feedback, and logs of no-shows or event disputes. This data is critical for maintaining accountability across the platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your data to operate, maintain, and improve the EVENTO ecosystem. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Facilitate verified coordination and communication between organizers and volunteers.
  • Track attendance and reliability metrics to prevent no-shows and build trustworthy matching algorithms.
  • Provide customer support and resolve payment or attendance disputes.
  • Ensure compliance with our safety rules and prevent malicious activity.

4. Information Sharing & Transparency

Because EVENTO is built on mutual accountability, certain profile information—such as your name, role, rating, and verified status—is visible to other registered users (e.g., event organizers see volunteer credentials when hiring, and volunteers see event organizer details). We never sell your data to third parties. We only share it when required by law or to resolve active disputes.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. To preserve platform integrity and ensure accountability (such as preventing users from bypassing low trust ratings by recreating accounts), certain historic reliability records (e.g., severe contract breaches or chronic no-shows) may be retained even after account closure, subject to applicable local regulations.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data. Depending on your location, you may request to access, correct, or delete your personal account information. Please note that data deletion requests will be evaluated to ensure they do not compromise the integrity of active disputes or history records that are essential for platform accountability.
